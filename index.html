About me

Shauna Hathaway McHugh

Hello, my name is Shauna and I'm an artist. I have been drawing for almost 23 years. I took interest in animation aswell at a very young age. In 2017, I began studying animation in Ballyfermot, Dublin. I studied for 3 years but it was unfortunate that covid made it impossible to keep up with but I was able to pass my studies. Now as a freelancer artist, I use Procreate on my iPad to keep up with my drawings. However, I still do traditional art too.
