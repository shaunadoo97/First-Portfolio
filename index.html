About Me

Shauna Hathaway McHugh

Hello, my name is Shauna and I'm an artist. I have been drawing for almost 23 years. I took interest in animation aswell at a very young age. In 2017, I began studying animation in Ballyfermot, Dublin. I studied for 3 years but it was unfortunate that covid made it impossible to keep up with but I was able to pass my studies. During the lockdown 2019, I began to take interest on doing digital work especially around comic-style type, also including the ability to include myself in tv shows.


How I do my Art Commissions

First, send me a preferred picture of yourself, your family/friends or anyone you know who would love to be drawn in this style or be drawn in movies/tv shows. I will do upa concept design first before going ahead to develop it more.

Once you're satisfied with my idea, I will proceed to go ahead and apply the first base with just colour (no details or shading just yet) and ensure you're still happy.

If still pleased, I will then go on to finalise the finishing product. See below for example. Of course, if any changes are needed that's okay but it will probably cause some time to do so as it takes time.
